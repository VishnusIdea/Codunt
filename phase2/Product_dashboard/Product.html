DUCATI

Ducati is an Italian motorcycle manufacturing company headquartered in Bologna, Italy. The company is directly owned by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini, whose German parent company is Audi, itself owned by the Volkswagen Group.

The Street Ninja is a sleek, agile bike designed for urban environments, blending speed and maneuverability for a smooth ride through city streets. Its compact frame and responsive handling make it perfect for navigating traffic and tight spaces with ease.

PULSAR
